Technical Support

Step 1: Open Settings

- Open Settings

- Click 'Safari' in Settings

- Click 'Extensions' in 'Safari'

- Find 'searchTools' and Enter

- Allow Extension

Step 2: Open Safari Browser

- Open Safari browser

- Tap the AA icon in the Address Bar

- Tap Manage extensions

- Find 'searchTools'

- Turn on

For Apple Offical Guide, visit the Apple Guide.
